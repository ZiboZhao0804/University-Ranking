A visualization of best universities in the United States

Description: In the Colleges layer, the locations of the top 800 colleges in the United States are shown in the map; in the States layer, the color corresponds to the number of top 800 colleges in each state. You may hover over on a specific state and click for a zoom in view. We observed a higher number of top colleges on the east side of the country.


15 states with the highest number of the top 800 universities

The bar plot shows the 15 states that have the highest number of the top 800 universities. New York, California, Pennsylvania, Texas, Massachusetts, and Illinois are the top 6 states.

SAT score distribution in different Niche grade categories

The box plot displays the range of SAT scores found in the 4 Niche categories for the top 800 colleges. We observe that schools with a higher SAT range area associated with a higher Niche Grade.
